Water Energy Resources

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Energy, Environmental & Chemical Engineering at Washington University in St. Louis. My research aims to understand interfacial processes that govern contaminant transformation and mineral reactivity in complex environments.

I use this mechanistic understanding to inform both water treatment and resource recovery. My work bridges fundamental mechanisms and engineering applications to address challenges at the energy–water–resource nexus.
